Privacy Policy

Prayer times depend on your location and we try to figure that out from your IP address. You can also enter your location on the settings page yourself and get more accurate times. Other than your location and standard http headers (your browser, IP etc.) there's a few other pieces of information you let us know. The calculation method you want to use, time offsets, adhan reciters for each prayer time, your custom alarms and so on. Anything you see on the settings pages and nothing you don't. We don't store your information permanently and never share your information with anyone.

We have no access to your computer or any other personal data. Your AdhanClock settings are stored on your browser's local storage.

We use a slightly improved version of praytimes.org js library for prayer times calculations.

For any questions or suggestions please contact us at adhanclock@gmail.com.
